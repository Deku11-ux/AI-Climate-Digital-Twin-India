# Project Scope

## Project Name
Bharat Climate Intelligence Twin (BCIT)

## Objective
To develop an AI-powered Climate Digital Twin of India that provides state-wise climate prediction, environmental monitoring, policy simulation, and decision support.

## Modules

- Climate Prediction
- Climate Risk Assessment
- Water Monitoring
- AQI Monitoring
- Biodiversity Monitoring
- Land Use Monitoring
- Adaptation Strategy Generator
- Mitigation Strategy Generator
- AI Climate Advisor
- Scenario Simulation

## Target Users

- Government of India
- State Governments
- Disaster Management Authorities
- Researchers
- Farmers
- Urban Planners

## Out of Scope

- Live IoT integration
- Mobile application
- Nationwide real-time deployment
